BENEFITS PACKAGE

HEALTH INSURANCE
EduTech Academy offers comprehensive health insurance coverage including medical, dental, and vision plans. The company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 50% for dependents. Plan year runs January to December.

HEALTH PLAN OPTIONS
Employees can choose between three health plans: HMO (lowest cost, limited network), PPO (higher cost, broader network), or HDHP (requires health savings account). Employees can change plans during open enrollment in November.

DENTAL INSURANCE
Dental coverage includes preventive care (cleanings, exams) at 100%, basic procedures at 80%, and major procedures at 50%. Annual dental maximum is $2000.

VISION INSURANCE
Vision coverage includes eye exams annually, and $200 allowance for eyeglasses or contact lenses every 2 years. Dependent coverage is available for children up to age 26.

LIFE INSURANCE
The company provides term life insurance equal to 2x annual salary at no cost to employees. Additional coverage can be purchased at employee expense. Coverage continues for 30 days after employment termination.

DISABILITY INSURANCE
Short-term disability covers 60% of salary for up to 12 weeks. Long-term disability covers 60% of salary after 12 weeks, up to age 65. Both are provided by the company at no cost.

401(K) RETIREMENT PLAN
Employees are eligible for the 401(k) plan after 3 months of employment. The company matches 50% of contributions up to 6% of salary. Plan administrator is Vanguard.

401(K) INVESTMENT OPTIONS
Employees can invest in mutual funds, target-date funds, or stable value funds. Company match vests over 3 years (33% per year). Employees can change allocations quarterly.

EMPLOYEE WELLNESS PROGRAM
The company provides a wellness program including gym membership discounts, mental health counseling (5 free sessions per year), wellness challenges, and health screenings.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT FUND
Each employee receives $2000 per year for professional development. Funds can be used for courses, certifications, conferences, or training. Approval required from manager.

TUITION REIMBURSEMENT
Employees pursuing degrees related to their role can receive up to $5000 per year in tuition reimbursement. A 2-year commitment to the company is required after graduation.

STOCK OPTION PROGRAM
Senior employees (manager level and above) may receive stock options as part of their compensation package. Options vest over 4 years. Details provided individually by HR.

HR POLICY: Employee Handbook

COMPANY OVERVIEW
EduTech Academy is an educational technology company providing online courses and learning solutions to students worldwide. Our mission is to democratize access to quality education through innovative digital platforms.

EQUAL OPPORTUNITY POLICY
EduTech Academy is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We are committed to building a diverse and inclusive workplace. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other legally protected status.

ANTI-DISCRIMINATION AND HARASSMENT POLICY
All employees have the right to work in an environment free from discrimination and harassment. Any form of harassment, bullying, or discrimination will not be tolerated. Employees who experience or witness harassment should report it immediately to HR.

RECRUITMENT AND HIRING
Our hiring process is designed to identify talented individuals who align with our company values. We recruit from diverse talent pools and ensure fair evaluation of all candidates. Background checks are conducted for all positions.

EMPLOYEE CLASSIFICATION
Full-Time Employees: Entitled to benefits, paid time off, and company benefits package.
Part-Time Employees: Work less than 30 hours per week, eligible for limited benefits.
Contract Employees: Hired for specific projects or durations, governed by individual contracts.
Interns: Non-paid or paid positions for educational experience, typically 3-6 months.

COMPENSATION AND SALARY REVIEW
Salaries are reviewed annually in March. Performance-based raises are determined by manager recommendations and HR review. Salary is confidential and should not be discussed publicly.

OVERTIME POLICY
Non-exempt employees are entitled to overtime pay at 1.5x regular rate for hours worked beyond 40 per week. Overtime must be approved by manager before being worked.

REMOTE WORK POLICY
Eligible employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Core hours are 10 AM to 3 PM in company timezone. Weekly team meetings are mandatory and conducted in-person or via video.

WORK FROM HOME SCHEDULE
Employees must maintain a regular work-from-home schedule and communicate it to their manager. Sudden changes should be notified 24 hours in advance when possible.

HOME OFFICE EQUIPMENT
The company provides a laptop and monitors for remote workers. Additional equipment requests must be approved by manager and submitted to IT. Personal equipment must meet company security standards.

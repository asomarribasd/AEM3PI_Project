COURSE ENROLLMENT AND REGISTRATION

ENROLLMENT PROCESS
Students can enroll in courses through the online portal. Select your course and click "Enroll". Complete the registration form with personal information. Confirm your email address to receive course access. Enrollment confirmation sent within 24 hours.

PAYMENT OPTIONS
Courses can be paid by credit card, debit card, or PayPal. Monthly payment plans available for courses over $500. Corporate group discounts available (contact sales). Scholarship programs available for eligible students.

REFUND POLICY
Full refund within 14 days of enrollment. 50% refund between 15-30 days. No refund after 30 days. Refund requests must include reason. Refunds processed within 10 business days.

COURSE SCHEDULE OPTIONS
Courses offered in multiple start dates throughout the year. Check the course page for available start dates. Self-paced options available for most courses. Cohort-based courses offer interaction with peers.

COURSE DELIVERY METHODS
Live online classes: Interactive sessions with instructor, fixed schedule. Self-paced: Complete at your own speed with flexible schedule. Hybrid: Mix of live sessions and self-paced content. Video-on-demand: Pre-recorded content available 24/7.

ACCESS TO COURSE MATERIALS
Upon enrollment, you receive access to video lectures, slides, and exercise files. Materials remain accessible for 1 year after course completion. Download options available for offline viewing. Discussion forums for peer interaction.

PREREQUISITES AND SKILL REQUIREMENTS
Check course prerequisites before enrolling. Prerequisites ensure you have required knowledge. If unsure about prerequisites, contact our admissions team. Placement quizzes available to assess readiness.

COURSE PREREQUISITES WAIVER
Exceptions to prerequisites may be granted. Submit a waiver request with your CV and background. Academic advisor will review your qualifications. Waivers granted on case-by-case basis.

GROUP AND CORPORATE ENROLLMENT
Organizations can enroll multiple employees in courses. Group discounts apply at 10+ enrollments. Dedicated support for group administration. Custom course schedules available for groups.

STUDENT RECORD AND TRANSCRIPT
Student records are maintained securely in our portal. Transcripts show course completion with grade. Official transcripts can be requested anytime. Digital credentials issued upon completion.

AUDITING COURSES
Audit option available for some courses at reduced price. Auditors receive course materials but no certificate. No grading or feedback for audits. Perfect for learning without certification.

COURSE TRANSFERS
Course enrollment can be transferred to different start date. Transfers must be requested before course starts. One free transfer allowed per course. Additional transfers subject to fees.

DROPPING A COURSE
Courses can be dropped anytime with appropriate refund (per refund policy). Notification of drop within 24 hours. Drop confirmation in student portal. After drop, course access is disabled.

GRADUATION REQUIREMENTS
Complete all course assignments and projects. Achieve minimum 70% on final assessment. Attend required live sessions (for cohort-based courses). Participate in peer reviews. Grades reviewed before certificate issuance.

CERTIFICATES AND CREDENTIALS
Digital certificates issued upon completion. Certificates display course name, completion date, and instructor name. Can be shared on LinkedIn and resume. Certificates verified through our portal.

FINANCIAL AID AND SCHOLARSHIPS
Need-based scholarships available. Merit scholarships for high-performing students. Military and veteran benefits. Contact financial aid office for more information.
